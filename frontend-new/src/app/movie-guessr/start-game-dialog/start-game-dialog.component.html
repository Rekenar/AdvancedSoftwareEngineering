How to play?

Guess the movie!

Welcome to the "Movie Guessr" Quiz Game! Challenge your movie knowledge by deciphering movie titles from hints. At the beginning of the game, you'll receive 10 coins to spend on revealing hints about the movie.

Keep in mind that hints can be surprisingly helpful or deceptively tricky. When you're confident in your guess, type it into the input field. If you guess correctly, you'll earn 5 coins as a reward for your movie prowess. However, if your guess misses the mark, you'll lose a coin. So, let's get started and see how many movies you can guess correctly!

If you loose all your coins, the game is over. Try and get as far as possible and set a new highscore!
